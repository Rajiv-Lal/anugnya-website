What Our Patients Experience

Five patients. Different cancers, different treatments, different points in the journey. Each illustrates what energy healing and complementary support change in practice. Patient names have been changed to protect confidentiality.

During Treatment
Patient 1 — Stage 4 Hodgkin's Lymphoma, age 21. Completed all chemotherapy cycles.
Hodgkin's Lymphoma Stage 4 · 21 years old
⌄

Patient 1 was 21 when diagnosed with Stage 4 Hodgkin's Lymphoma. Twice-daily remote energy healing sessions were provided alongside chemotherapy cycles.

The tingling and pins-and-needles sensation in the limbs (chemotherapy-induced peripheral neuropathy), and the burning heat sensation — both were controlled through the course of treatment. Nausea appeared later in the cycles but was reversed through energy healing and product support.

Side effects managedNeuropathy, burning, nausea
Treatment completedEvery cycle, without interruption
Current statusIn remission
During Treatment
Patient 2 — Glioblastoma Stage 4, age 65. Liver and blood markers tracked over 6 months.
Glioblastoma Stage 4 · 65 years old · Post brain surgery
⌄

Patient 2 was 65. Following brain surgery for Glioblastoma, he underwent radiation and chemotherapy for six months. Mid-treatment, a COVID infection caused acute liver damage on top of already-declining blood counts.

Over that six-month period, three markers were tracked. Haemoglobin, suppressed by chemotherapy, recovered steadily. Lymphocyte counts — the marker of immune function — returned progressively to normal range. Liver function, which spiked acutely in June following the COVID infection, normalised completely within a month.

Toxicity gradeGrade 1–2 throughout (benchmark: 35% at Grade 3+)
Liver functionNormalised within 1 month of COVID spike
HaemoglobinSteady recovery throughout treatment
During Treatment
Patient 3 — Intestinal Cancer on chemotherapy. Three side effects managed with product support.
Intestinal Cancer · On chemotherapy
⌄

Patient 3 experienced three side effects that disrupted his daily function: tingling and pins-and-needles in the hands and feet, a burning heat sensation in the limbs, and deep bone and muscle pain common in chemotherapy patients.

Three products addressed each directly: a Himalayan salt foot soak for the tingling and burning, an essential oil massage oil for bone and muscle pain and fatigue, and an active soap for skin that had become very dry and brittle under treatment.

"I had gone through 2 cycles of chemo treatment. I am doing well so far. No major side effects. I am using the soap and moringa oil regularly and the salt bath occasionally." — Patient 3
After Treatment
Patient 4 — Cancer Survivor. Joined the meditation programme post-treatment.
Cancer Survivor · Meditation Programme
⌄

Patient 4 joined Anugnya's meditation programme as a survivor, past the acute phase of treatment. The sessions gave him a reframe — life organised around recovery rather than defined by cancer.

"It was a magical experience going through the meditation session today. I will encourage people in the group to join and take blessings." — Patient 4
Caregiver
Patient 5 — Caregiver. Wife diagnosed with breast cancer, both supported through the journey.
Wife diagnosed with breast cancer
⌄

Raj joined Anugnya's meditation group when his wife was diagnosed with breast cancer. His wife completed treatment. She is in remission.

After her remission, Raj continued attending. Not because she needed him to, but because the thirty minutes he spent in the group each day had become something he needed for himself — a dedicated space that was his alone.
